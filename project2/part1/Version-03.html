Hope is the thing with feathers                                   And sweetest in the gale is heard;                                I've heard it in the chillest land,
That perches in the soul,                                             And sore must be the storm                                         And on the strangest sea;
And sings the tune without the words,                       That could abash the little bird                                     Yet, never, in extremity,
And never stops at all,                                                That kept so many warm.                                              It asked a crumb of me.
